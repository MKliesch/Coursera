Description

This Shiny App shows you which places in Switzerland can be reached directly from Bern or Zurich. It is aimed at people who work in either of the two but prefer living outside of larger cities.

Instruction

The Population Size allows you to filter out places above or below a certain population size.

The Number of Connections Per Hour setting limits the places shown to those that have a certain number of train or bus connections to Zurich or Bern per hour.

Travel Time in Minutes is the setting to define the minimum and maximum travel time from Zurich or Bern. Only places that have connections within that time range will be shown.

The selection Bern/ZÃ¼rich allows you to select whether you want to be shown places that have connections to Zurich, to Bern or to both.

The weekday selection allows you to specify the days that are included in the calculation of the number of connections per hour. I.e. if only Sunday is ticked and Number of Connections per Hour is set to 1-10, only places are shown that have between 1-10 connections to Zurich or Bern on a Sunday .
